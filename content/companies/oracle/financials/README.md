# Oracle Corporation — Financials

## Fiscal calendar

Oracle's fiscal year ends **May 31**.

| Fiscal quarter | Calendar months | Example (FY2026) |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Jun – Aug | Jun 1, 2025 – Aug 31, 2025 |
| Q2 | Sep – Nov | Sep 1, 2025 – Nov 30, 2025 |
| Q3 | Dec – Feb | Dec 1, 2025 – Feb 28, 2026 |
| Q4 | Mar – May | Mar 1, 2026 – May 31, 2026 |

## Filename convention

`FYyyyy-Qn.yaml` — e.g. `FY2026-Q3.yaml` for the quarter ending February 28, 2026.

## SEC filing pattern

- **10-Q** for Q1, Q2, Q3
- **10-K** for Q4 (full fiscal year)
- CIK: `0001341439`
- Company facts: `https://data.sec.gov/api/xbrl/companyfacts/CIK0001341439.json`

## Sources

Every material number backed by `sources` (URL, document kind, optional excerpt) in the quarter YAML.
